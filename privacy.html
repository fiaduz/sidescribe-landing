Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

At SideScribe, we believe that your research and notes are your own. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our browser extension and website.

1. Data Collection

We aim to collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to provide our service:

  • User Content: We store the text highlights, URLs, and notes you explicitly save using the extension.
  • Account Info: If you create an account, we store your email address and basic profile information.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous technical logs (browser type, extension version) to help us squash bugs.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide the SideScribe experience. Specifically:

  • To sync your highlights across different devices.
  • To improve the extension's performance and user interface.
  • We never sell your personal data or browsing history to third-party advertisers.

3. Storage & Security

Your data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS protocols. While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. By using SideScribe, you acknowledge these inherent risks.

4. Third-Party Services

We value your privacy and maintain a strict "no-sharing" policy regarding your personal data. The only exception is our payment processor:

  • Razorpay: We use Razorpay for secure payment processing. When you upgrade your account, your payment information is handled directly by Razorpay.
  • Data Minimization: Aside from this essential transaction processing, SideScribe does not share, sell, or rent any user data, highlights, or browsing history to any third-party companies or advertisers.
